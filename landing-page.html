Questions

Is my voice data really private?
Yes. MurmurTone processes everything on your computer using local AI models. Your voice recordings and transcriptions never leave your machine—there's no server to send them to.
Do I need an internet connection?
Only for the initial model download (~75MB-1.5GB) and one-time license activation. After that, MurmurTone works completely offline.
