Frequently Asked Questions

Is my voice data stored anywhere?
No. MurmurTone processes everything locally on your machine. Your voice audio is never uploaded to any server, never stored in any cloud, and never leaves your computer. Once transcription is complete, the audio is discarded.
Do I need internet to use MurmurTone?
No. MurmurTone works completely offline. The only time you need internet is for the initial license activation (one-time) and to download larger speech models if you choose them. After that, everything runs locally.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
After your trial ends, you'll be prompted to purchase a Pro license ($49/year). If you don't purchase, the app will stop working until you activate a license. Your settings and preferences are preserved.
What's included in the $49/year license?
Everything! Unlimited transcription, all speech models, AI text cleanup (with Ollama), translation mode, audio file transcription, custom vocabulary, voice commands, and priority support. No hidden fees or per-minute charges.
How does AI text cleanup work without cloud?
MurmurTone integrates with Ollama, which runs AI models locally on your machine. When enabled, your transcription is cleaned up by a local LLM‚Äîgrammar fixes, formality adjustments‚Äîall without sending any data to external servers.
Will my price increase after Early Access?
No. When you purchase during Early Access at $49/year, that rate is locked in forever. Future users may pay more, but your renewal rate stays the same as long as you maintain your subscription.
