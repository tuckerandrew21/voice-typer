Pricing Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal through our payment partner LemonSqueezy. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes! If you're not satisfied within 30 days of purchase, contact us for a full refund. No questions asked. We want you to be completely happy with MurmurTone.
What happens when my subscription expires?
If you don't renew, the app will stop working after your subscription period ends. Your settings and preferences are preserved, so if you renew later, everything will be exactly as you left it.
Can I use MurmurTone on multiple computers?
Your license key can be activated on up to 2 computers at a time. If you need more, contact us for volume licensing options.
Will the price increase after Early Access?
Prices may increase for new customers after Early Access ends, but your rate is locked in forever. If you purchase at $49/year during Early Access, that's your renewal rate for life.
Do I need to pay for Ollama separately?
No. Ollama is free and open-source software. MurmurTone integrates with it for AI text cleanup, but there's no additional cost. You just need to install Ollama and download a model (also free).
