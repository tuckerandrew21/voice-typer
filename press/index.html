About MurmurTone

MurmurTone is privacy-first voice-to-text software for Windows that processes speech 100% locally. Unlike cloud-based alternatives that send your voice to remote servers, MurmurTone runs entirely on your computer using AI speech recognition. Your voice never leaves your machine.

Launched in 2025, MurmurTone addresses growing privacy concerns in an age of pervasive cloud services. It offers fast, accurate transcription with features like AI text cleanup, translation mode, and voice commands - all running offline.

Key Messages

  • Privacy-First: All speech recognition happens locally on your computer. Voice data is never transmitted to any server.
  • Completely Offline: After initial setup, MurmurTone works without any internet connection.
  • Powered by Whisper: Uses OpenAI's Whisper speech recognition model, running locally via faster-whisper.
  • Affordable: $49/year vs. competitors charging $96-180/year for cloud-based alternatives.
  • Feature-Rich: AI text cleanup, translation mode, voice commands, audio/video file transcription.
  • No Account Required: No sign-up, no cloud account, no data collection. Just download and use.
