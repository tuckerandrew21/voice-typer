Quick Start

Get started with MurmurTone in under 5 minutes:

  1. Download and Install

    Download the installer from murmurtone.com/install and run it. Follow the on-screen instructions.

  2. Launch MurmurTone

    Find MurmurTone in your system tray (bottom-right of your taskbar). The icon looks like a shield with a waveform.

  3. Configure Your Hotkey

    Right-click the tray icon → Settings → Click "Set Hotkey" and press your preferred key combination. Default: Ctrl+Shift+Space

  4. Start Transcribing

    Press and hold your hotkey, speak clearly, then release. Your transcribed text will appear in the active window.

First-time setup The first transcription may take 30-60 seconds while the speech model loads into memory. Subsequent transcriptions will be instant.

Basic Usage

Recording and Transcribing

MurmurTone uses a "push-to-talk" system:

  1. Press your hotkey to start recording
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone
  3. Release the hotkey to stop recording and transcribe
  4. Your text appears in the active application

Audio feedback sounds indicate the current state:

  • Beep (start): Recording has begun
  • Processing sound: Transcription in progress
  • Success chime: Transcription complete
  • Error sound: Something went wrong

System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 (build 19041+) or Windows 11
  • RAM: 4 GB minimum (8 GB recommended)
  • Disk: 500 MB free space
  • Microphone: Any USB or built-in microphone
  • Optional: NVIDIA GPU with CUDA for faster transcription
