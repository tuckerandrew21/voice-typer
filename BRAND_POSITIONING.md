# MurmurTone - Brand Positioning

## Brand Name
**MurmurTone** - Private, Local Voice-to-Text for Windows

## Tagline
"Your voice, locally."

## Brand Promise
MurmurTone empowers professionals to capture their thoughts instantly without sacrificing privacy. Work faster, think clearer, own your words—all offline.

---

## Core Values

### Privacy First
- 100% offline processing
- No cloud uploads, no data tracking
- Your voice never leaves your machine

### Simplicity
- One hotkey, instant transcription
- No configuration required out-of-the-box
- Lightweight, runs quietly in the background

### Reliability
- Local processing = no internet dependency
- Consistent performance every time
- Works anywhere, anytime

---

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Knowledge workers (writers, programmers, researchers)
- Professionals who value privacy
- Remote workers and freelancers
- Anyone tired of cloud-dependent tools

### Secondary
- People with accessibility needs
- Non-native English speakers (with auto-detect mode)
- Power users who want control over their tools

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **Truly Local** - No cloud, no lag, no tracking unlike Otter.ai, Rev, or Google Docs voice typing
2. **Privacy-by-Default** - Not a feature, it's the foundation
3. **Lightweight** - Minimal system resources compared to alternatives
4. **Customizable** - Choose your model (speed vs accuracy), hotkey, language settings
5. **Open Architecture** - Built on faster-whisper, extensible for power users
